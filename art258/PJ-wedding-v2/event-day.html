Event Day

Location

The ceremony and reception will be at Ko‘olau Ballrooms & Conference Center, located on the island of O‘ahu. The venue sits on top of a lush golf course surrounded by a sprawling mountain range. When you arrive, look for a sign that says "Ko‘olau Golf Club."

Directions

The venue is a roughly 30-minute drive from the airport, or 45-minute drive from Waikiki, but please allow extra time in case of traffic.

H3 testing

From the Airport (30 min)

Take H-3 Freeway and cruise through the mountains. Exit Kamehameha Highway (Route 83). Turn left onto Kamehameha Highway, and at the first stop sign, turn left onto Kionaole Rd. Follow the road all the way to the parking lot.

From Waikiki (45 min)

Take H-1 West, and exit at Pali Highway (Route 61). After winding through the mountains and exiting the last tunnel, turn left onto Kamehameha Highway (Route 83). Turn left at the first stop sign, and follow the road all the way to the parking lot.

Schedule

Our big day begins with a short, standing-room* ceremony outside followed by cocktail hour and dinner indoors, all over a sweeping sunset view of the Ko‘olau Mountains. *Limited seats will be reserved for immediate family, and those with mobility issues and young children.

4:00 pm

Ceremony

Meet us outside on the 10th tee where we make it official.

5:00 pm

Cocktail Hour

Head inside for cocktails and photo opportunities galore.

6:00 pm

Reception

Enjoy a delicious buffet dinner and drinks. There will be games and prizes, dancing, and a photobooth!

10:00 pm

Closing Time

As much fun as we've had, it's time to call it a night. Thank you so much for making our big day special!
